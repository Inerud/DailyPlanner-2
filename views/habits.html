    How to get started:

  • Set your habits by clicking on +.
  • Set a monthly goal for each habit.
  • Click on any empty block in the calendar to mark a habit as done. Click again to toggle.
  • Change the name or goal of a habit anytime by clicking on the cell you want to edit.

    Tips:

  • Don't try to start up many new habits all at once, trying to change to much of your lifestyle at once can cause you to get overwhelmed and makes it harder to stick to them
  • Habits are formed by repeating an action consistently in the same context. For example eating a piece of fruit after breakfast, or doing some streches after getting out of bed.
  • When starting habit tracking, start by adding in things you already do regularly.
  • Habits should be formed for life, until they become like brushing teeth.
  • Goals should be flexible. You don’t need to try and maintain streaks. Just set a realistic and achievable goal and try to achieve it.
